Truth or Dare is one of the all-time classic party games—and a great way to get to know your friends better The premise is simple: Players take turns asking one another "truth or dare?" If they choose truth, they have to answer a question of the asker's choosing. If they choose dare, the asker dares them to do something rather than make a confession. Good truth or dare questions are thought-provoking, and some can elicit just as much of an adrenaline rush as doing a dare.
The key to a good truth or dare question?
Make it something that will catch the player somewhat off-guard and ask something the rest of the players would be generally interested in learning.
